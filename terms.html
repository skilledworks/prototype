Thank you for choosing Skilled Works. These Terms and Conditions (“Terms”) outline our working relationship clearly, openly, and respectfully.

1. Introduction

Skilled Works, operated by Roman Stachurski, provides specialist services focused primarily on the professional handling, installation, and display of artworks. Roman Stachurski aims for exceptional quality and client satisfaction at all times.

2. Definitions

  • “Client,” “You,” or “Your” refers to the individual, organisation, or entity engaging Skilled Works.
  • “Roman Stachurski,” “Service Provider,” “We,” or “Our” refers to Skilled Works.
  • “Services” or “Work” refers to the agreed-upon tasks undertaken by Skilled Works.

  • “Materials” refers to any products purchased specifically to complete the Work.

3. Scope of Services

Services provided by Skilled Works include, but are not limited to:

  • Art hanging and installation
  • Transport and careful handling of artwork
  • Custom packaging for storage or transport
  • Guidance on framing and artwork installation

Any specific services requested will be agreed upon before commencement.

4. Estimates, Fees, and Payments

  • A call-out fee may apply if travel and consultation occur without subsequent agreement to perform work.
  • Payment terms and methods will be stated clearly on your invoice. Prompt payment upon job completion is appreciated.
